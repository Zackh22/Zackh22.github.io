Custom Built Dungeons & Dragons Table

About Dungeons and Dragons:

Project Overview:

This is a custom built table that my friends and I play D&D on. The table has a strand of LEDs attached to it. These LEDs are used to simulate different lighting and weather effects surrounding the characters. As an added bonus, when the characters enters combat, the lights transform into a visual health bars that displays the characters' current hit points.
